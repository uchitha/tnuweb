Little About us

TN'U pty ltd is a privately owned small company run from South of the river, Perth. We are a group of industry professionals each having more than 10 years of experience developing business solutions and a whole life time of computing experience.


Having seen how the computers have evolved from being a geek kids toy to a point where they are taking over our lives, we feel the common public is exposed to risks and challenges that they don't fully understand.

Our attempt is to get involved and solve the nagging computing issues you have and in the process to educate you and your household or your business on how to instill some best practices so that you continue to have an enjoyable computing experience.
